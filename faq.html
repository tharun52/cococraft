Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of products do you sell?
We sell a wide range of handcrafted artifacts inspired by the rich cultural heritage of Kerala, including traditional art forms, home decor items, and accessories made by local artisans.
Our products include hand-painted murals, wooden sculptures, traditional clothing, and much more.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we offer international shipping for all our products. Customers from around the world can enjoy our handcrafted items.
We ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times may vary depending on the destination.
Can I return or exchange a product?
Yes, we have a hassle-free return and exchange policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return or exchange it within 30 days.
Please make sure the product is unused and in its original packaging. Contact our customer support team for assistance with returns and exchanges.
Are your products eco-friendly?
Yes, sustainability is important to us. Our products are made from natural materials and we strive to minimize our environmental impact.
We work closely with local artisans who use eco-friendly techniques and materials. Our packaging is also biodegradable.
