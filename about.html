Welcome to CocoCraft Creations!

At CocoCraft Creations, we believe in the beauty of nature and the magic of handmade artistry. Our mission is to bring you exquisite, eco-friendly products crafted from coconut shells, each piece uniquely designed to enhance your home and lifestyle.

Our Story

CocoCraft Creations was born in Kerala, Thrissur out of a passion for sustainable living and a love for handcrafted goods. We saw the potential in coconut shells, a natural resource that is often discarded, and transformed them into beautiful, functional items. Our journey began with a small team of artisans dedicated to showcasing the natural elegance of coconut shells through their craftsmanship.

Our Products

Every product at CocoCraft Creations tells a story of meticulous craftsmanship and sustainable practices. From our elegant coconut shell tea cups to charming plant pots and whimsical toys, each item is handcrafted with care. We offer a wide range of products, including:

  • Kitchen Accessories: Coconut shell spoons, cups, and serving spoons that add a touch of natural beauty to your dining experience.
  • Home Decor: Unique candle holders and plant pots that bring a piece of nature into your home.
  • Toys Delightful coconut shell toys that are safe, natural, and fun for children.
